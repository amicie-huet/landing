Rajasthan, the land of kings

Have you ever heard about Rajasthan ? This region is situated in the West of India, at the border with Pakistan. It is nicknamed the "Land of Kings" as it was home to the powerful Mughal empire in the 16th and 17th centuries.

You will discover some of the most fascinating cities of the world by visiting impressive fortresses or magnificent palaces, wandering through colorful and buzzling streets, meeting the kindest people and eating delicious food.

If you have ever dreamt of going into the unknown and learning about a completely different culture, this is your sign !

Gems of Rajasthan

Jaipur

Wander through the streets of the pink city and discover the mythic Fort Amber.

Udaipur

Take in the ethereal beauty of the Lake city, which served as decor for many movies.

Ranakpur

Make a stop at the jain temple of Ranakpur and admire all these handmade marble carvings.

Jodhpur

Discover the fort of the Blue City in a more adventurous way by ziplining around it.
